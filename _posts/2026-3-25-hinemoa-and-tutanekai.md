# 🛶💖 Hinemoa and Tūtānekai 💖🛶

On the shores of Lake Rotorua, there was a village. In that village lived **Hinemoa**, a chief’s daughter, and **Tūtānekai**, a young man of great skill and kindness. They heard each other’s music across the lake and fell in love.

But Tūtānekai lived on Mokoia Island in the middle of the lake, and Hinemoa’s family did not want her to marry him.

---

## 🎶 A Love Across the Water

Every night, Tūtānekai played his flute on the island. The sound drifted across the water to Hinemoa’s village. Hinemoa would sit by the shore and listen, her heart aching.

“I must find a way to reach him,” she whispered.

But her family watched her closely. They removed all the canoes from the shore so she could not paddle across.

---

## 🌙 A Brave Journey

One night, Hinemoa could bear it no longer. She decided to swim.

She wrapped empty gourds around her body to help her float. Then she slipped into the dark water and began to swim towards the island.

The water was cold. The lake was wide. Her arms grew tired, but she thought of Tūtānekai and kept going.

Hours passed. Finally, her feet touched the bottom near Mokoia Island.

---

## 🛁 A Clever Disguise

Hinemo a was exhausted and shivering. She found a hot pool (a natural hot spring) on the island and climbed in to warm herself.

Tūtānekai’s servant came to fetch water. In the darkness, he saw a figure in the pool and called out, “Who are you?”

“I am just a woman,” Hinemoa replied. “But tell Tūtānekai that Hinemoa is here.”

The servant ran to Tūtānekai. When Tūtānekai heard the name, he rushed to the pool. There, in the warm water, he found Hinemoa.

Their joy was so great that the whole island celebrated.

---

## 💡 A Love That Overcame

Hinemoa and Tūtānekai were married, and her family accepted their love. Their story is still told as a reminder:

> True love takes courage and determination.  
> Sometimes we must overcome obstacles to be with those we care about.  
> Kindness and music can bring hearts together.

## 📖 Quick Glossary

| Word | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **gourd** | a hollowed-out fruit used as a container or float |
| **obstacle** | something that stands in your way |
| **determination** | strong will to keep trying |
| **hot spring** | a pool of naturally warm water, often from underground volcanoes |
