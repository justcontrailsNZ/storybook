# 🌊✨ Whaitere the Enchanted Stingray ✨🌊

Whaitere was no ordinary stingray. She was a magical creature who lived in the deep ocean. Her skin sparkled with light, and she could swim faster than any fish.

But Whaitere was also lonely. She longed to see the world above the waves.

---

## 🌕 A Journey to the Surface

One night, when the moon was full, Whaitere swam up, up, up until she broke the surface. She saw the stars, the moon, and the land in the distance. She was amazed.

She swam closer to the shore, where a village stood. People saw her glowing in the water and were frightened. They threw stones and called out warnings.

Whaitere was hurt, both by the stones and by their fear. She dived deep and did not return.

---

## 🧙 The Spirit’s Curse

An old tohunga (priest) saw what had happened. He felt sorry for Whaitere. He went to the shore and called her by name.

“Whaitere, do not be angry with the people,” he said. “They did not understand.”

He placed a blessing on her so that she would always find safe passage through the sea. To this day, it is said that stingrays swim with grace and are never harmed by those who respect them.

---

## 💡 What This Story Teaches

This story teaches:

> Fear can make us treat others unfairly.  
> Kindness and understanding can heal wounds.  
> All creatures deserve respect.
