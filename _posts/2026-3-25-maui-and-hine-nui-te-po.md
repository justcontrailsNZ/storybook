# ☠️🔥 Māui and Hine-nui-te-pō 🔥☠️

Māui had done many great things. He had slowed the sun, fished up islands, and brought fire to people. But there was one thing no one could escape—death. Māui wanted to change that. He wanted to make people live forever.

He knew that the goddess of death, **Hine-nui-te-pō**, held the secret. Her body was the gateway between life and death. If Māui could pass through her, death would be destroyed forever.

---

## 🐦 A Warning

Māui told his brothers about his plan. “I will crawl through Hine-nui-te-pō while she sleeps. When I emerge on the other side, death will be no more.”

His brothers were afraid. “This is too dangerous,” they said.

But Māui laughed. “I have conquered the sun and the sea. I can conquer death.”

He gathered his brothers and set out to find the goddess.

---

## 😴 The Sleeping Goddess

They found Hine-nui-te-pō lying in a deep sleep. Her body was huge, like a mountain. Māui explained his plan: “When I crawl inside her, you must not laugh. If you laugh, she will wake, and I will fail.”

Māui prepared himself. He took off his clothes and began to crawl towards the sleeping goddess. His heart pounded, but he was determined.

---

## 😂 The Fateful Laugh

As Māui crept closer, his brothers watched. They were nervous, but one of them could not hold back. The sight of Māui sneaking was too strange, and a small laugh escaped.

Then another brother laughed. Soon they were all giggling and pointing.

The laughter woke Hine-nui-te-pō. Her eyes snapped open.

She saw Māui. In an instant, she closed her legs, crushing him. Māui was caught, and he could not escape. He died then and there.

---

## 💡 The End of Immortality

With Māui’s death, the chance for humans to live forever was lost. From that day, all people must die.

This story teaches us:

> Even the bravest can fall if they do not listen to warnings.  
> Death is part of life, and trying to change that can have great cost.  
> Humility and respect are important, even for heroes.
